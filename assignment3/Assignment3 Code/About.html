Muscle Anatomy
An interactive guide to the human body

This website serves as a way for users to view, interact with, and learn about the anatomy of the human muscle structure. Whether you are an athlete, a regular at the gym, or the average joe it's important to know how and why we move. Additionally, its important to understand what can be done to strengthen and relieve these various components that make up our musculoskeltal system.

I am not a medical expert, nor a student in the field of physiology, and nothing found on this site should be taken as absolute. Please, whether it is found on this site, or the next, always advocate for yourself and do your own research. While I do not intend for this site to perpetuate false information or provide incorrect assessments of exercises and stretches, I can and will make mistakes that may lead to the presentation of incorrect information. So, once again, please do your own research and treat this as nothing more than an educated guide. - Braden Hicklin
